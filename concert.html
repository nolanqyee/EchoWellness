About This Year's Concert

This year, EchoWellness is dedicating its fundraising efforts to support veterans, a cause close to our hearts. We recognize the immense sacrifices made by these brave men and women, and we are committed to giving back to those who have served our country. The proceeds from our fundraising initiatives will be donated to the impactful organizations Purple Heart Homes and Veterans of Foreign Wars.

Purple Heart Homes is renowned for its work in providing housing solutions for service-connected disabled veterans, ensuring they have a safe and accessible place to live. Similarly, Veterans of Foreign Wars offers crucial support through advocacy, financial grants, and resources to improve the lives of veterans and their families.

This year, our fundraising goal is $20,000! By supporting to these esteemed organizations, EchoWellness aims to honor and assist veterans in meaningful and impactful ways.

2023 Concert Photos

In 2023, through our fundraising concert, we raised over $14,000 for the Journey School for the Uniquely Gifted and Talented, a special needs school that serves students with unique educational needs. With these funds, the Journey School was able to purchase new, accessible playground equipment for their students and bolster their educational experiences through more accessible programs and technology.

Thank you to all of those that attended our concert and donated to our cause!
